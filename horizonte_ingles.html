Horizon

The movement of the stars is given as an effect of its revolution; it modifies in every instant each impassable particle of all the imaginable. It waits behind the mystery to reveal you in everything an illusion. To exit it is needed to submerge. In the middle of this tempestuous ocean, the clarity is given in the abyss.

Every imperceptible lightning moves the inanimate. In your sight you will observe the effect of a flame that causes your vision. There are only tectonic-dimensional layers that beyond of the conceivable make the simulacrum of the unity to life’s celebration. The harmony of the beings where there is no privilege; each equidistant point to the perfection of the unknown.

What it is calling you is not what you perceive, nor a person, nor an entity, it is a sound that when understood is your secret. If you listen this universal whisper, move without looking back, that nothing lasted there. It is impossible to grasp this parallel plane to the existence without reflection, but reciprocal.

The identity is not the reflection, to realize is the abandon of the self. This is the defense of life, its purity. What emerges from here is indeterminate, without form. If you battle it, it expands. The principle of peace is given in the identity of the visible and the invisible.

Lauro Kräger
